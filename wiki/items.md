## Unique Items

Exactly three unique chase items exist in `0.3.1`.

| Unique | Slot | Stats |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Thorn of the Prior | Weapon | ATK +9, HP +4, Sight +2 |
| Ashen Vestments | Armor | DEF +6, HP +12, Sight +2, Fire Ward +2 |
| Crownshard Rosary | Charm | ATK +3, DEF +3, HP +6, Sight +2, Poison Ward +1, Fire Ward +1 |

The final boss reward chest grants one of these three at random.

Normal enemies can now drop a unique as an ultra-rare jackpot, but it is rare enough that the final boss remains the reliable premium source.
