# Merchant And Economy

## Merchant Rules

- Merchants appear from the `Broker's Lantern` route and can also appear rarely on normal floors.
- Each merchant carries exactly `5` offers.
- Every merchant always sells:
  - one healing consumable
  - one weapon
  - one armor piece
  - one charm
  - one extra slot that can be utility, another piece of gear, or a key

## Economy Shape

- Gold drops scale with floor depth.
- `Gilded Way` and `Cursed Procession` both increase gold gain.
- Brigands still steal gold, which now matters because shop visits are more powerful.
- Elite kills and boss kills produce larger gold spikes.

## Price Bands

| Item Tier | Typical Price |
| --- | --- |
| Common consumable | 18-32 gold |
| Uncommon consumable | 28-40 gold |
| Rare consumable | 58-96 gold |
| Common gear | 20-40 gold |
| Uncommon gear | 36-60 gold |
| Rare gear | 82-108 gold |
| Legendary gear | 124-144 gold |
| Bronze / Silver / Gold key | 28 / 56 / 108 gold |

## Practical Use

- Early merchants stabilize a weak build.
- Midrun merchants often bridge a failed loot streak.
- Late merchants can sell Legendary-tier upgrades that matter against floors 15-20.
