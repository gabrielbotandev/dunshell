# Loot And Rarities

## Rarity Ladder

| Rarity | Color | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Common | Pale bone | Early floor stability |
| Uncommon | Moss green | Midrun upgrades and mixed-stat gear |
| Rare | Blue steel | Strong directional upgrades |
| Legendary | Gold ember | High-end chase pieces and premium merchant stock |
| Unique | Blood-rose | Final-boss-only chase items |

## Drop Rules

- Ground loot uses floor-weighted rarity rolls.
- Route modifiers can push rarity upward.
- Elites boost item-drop chance.
- Boss reward chests bias toward stronger loot than normal floors.
- Unique items do not appear in normal loot tables.

## Reward Sources

| Source | Typical Output |
| --- | --- |
| Ground loot | Consumables or standard equipment |
| Enemy drops | Gold, occasional items, occasional keys |
| Normal chests | Gold plus 1-2 rewards |
| Boss chests | Large gold spikes and premium loot |
| Merchant stock | Curated five-offer shop inventory |
