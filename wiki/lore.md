# Lore

## The Abbey

Dunshell is set beneath a drowned abbey that buried itself rather than surrender the relic it kept. The upper halls flooded first. The lower sanctums learned to live on ash, bells, and prayers repeated long after the mouths that spoke them rotted away.

## The Cinder Crown

The `Cinder Crown` is not a royal treasure in any ordinary sense. It is a rite engine, a relic that once turned devotion into heat. The abbey feared what would happen if the crown were worn, broken, sold, or simply remembered, so it sank its own vaults around it.

## The Keepers

- `Houndmaster Vey` keeps the kennel reliquary through chains and obedience.
- `Bell Archivist Oria` protects the drowned record of the abbey's funerary tolls.
- `Censer Matriarch` turns devotion into poison and smoke.
- `Ashen Prior` still kneels over the crown as though liturgy could keep it buried.

## Endless Depths

Taking the crown does not end the abbey. It simply proves the upper twenty floors were never the bottom. Endless mode represents the deeper ruin: halls that only open once the relic is stolen and the abbey no longer has a reason to hide its worst rooms.
